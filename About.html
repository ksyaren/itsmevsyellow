Hello,

I love eating. Actually, I'm in love with it. I love eating everything that tastes nice.

At first, I enjoyed trying, eating, cooking and baking only delicious but sweet recipes.

Over time, I've realized that tasting nice doesn't mean eating nice.

However, eating nicely is not something we can easily get in this life, just like a happy life.

When I've realized I want both of them, my cooking journey has begun!

Now I'm cooking as I like, which makes me feel free.

The recipes that I try and share with you will be tasty, healthy and delicious; I promise.

Hoping you enjoy every single one of them as much as I do.

After all, we all want the same thing: eat nicely, live happily, feel free.
